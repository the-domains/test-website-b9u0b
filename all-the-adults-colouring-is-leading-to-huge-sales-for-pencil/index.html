All the adults colouring is leading to huge sales for pencil companies

SYDNEY, Australia - So many adults are spending their days colouring in the lines, pencil companies are struggling to cope with the demand for coloured pencils and felt tip pens. Staedtler , the German company which produces felt tip pens and coloured pencils, has seen significant growth in Australia as adults get obsessed with the mindful trend.
